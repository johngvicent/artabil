Our Mission & Vision

ArtÁbil is dedicated to the social and educational impact of analyzing accessibility in masterpieces. We believe that art history should be experienced by everyone, regardless of their visual capabilities.

We empower curators, educators, and developers to create a more inclusive world of art through data-driven simulation and technical rigor. By identifying accessibility barriers in classic works, we can develop better descriptive audio, tactile exhibits, and digital adaptations.
